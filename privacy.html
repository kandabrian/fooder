Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Welcome to Saffron & Salt ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you subscribe to our newsletter. This may include:

  • Your email address (if you subscribe to our newsletter)
  • Any feedback or comments you choose to send us

We also automatically collect certain information when you visit our website, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, and pages visited. This information is collected through standard web server logs and analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Send you our weekly recipe newsletter, if you have subscribed
  • Analyse how visitors use our site so we can improve it
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Respond to your enquiries or comments

3. Advertising & Third-Party Services

Saffron & Salt displays advertisements served by Adsterra and potentially other third-party advertising networks. These advertisers may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to serve ads that are relevant to your interests based on your browsing activity across websites.

We do not control the data collection practices of these third-party advertisers. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third parties. You may opt out of interest-based advertising by visiting the Digital Advertising Alliance at optout.aboutads.info or the Network Advertising Initiative at optout.networkadvertising.org.

4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies — small text files placed on your device — for the following purposes:

  • Analytics: To understand how visitors interact with our site
  • Advertising: Third-party advertisers may place cookies to serve relevant ads and measure performance
  • Functionality: To remember your preferences where applicable

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, some features of our website may not function properly without cookies.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist us in operating our website (such as email newsletter platforms), under confidentiality agreements
  • If required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Saffron & Salt or others

6. Data Retention

We retain your email address for as long as you remain subscribed to our newsletter. You may unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any newsletter email, after which your email address will be removed from our mailing list.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
